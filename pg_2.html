This is a tough one to get done in the required 10 minutes. The battle quickly gets very hectic; in addition to a bunch of Lizalfos and enemy warriors, before long you'll have to deal with four The Imprisoned. You can use any warrior you want but using Link with his Great Fairy is a good choice as it's great at clearing crowds.

There really isn't any trick to this one other than to make sure you're constantly downing enemies. Focus on the minions as you don't have time for the enemy warriors. Also, keep an eye on the positions of The Imprisoned. If you get cornered, you're going to take a lot of damage (plus, you often can't see where you are when The Imprisoned is in the way).
