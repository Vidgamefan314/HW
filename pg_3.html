Downloadable content

The Hero of Hyrule Season Pass is available on the Nintendo eShop for $19.99. Purchasing this gets you the Master Quest Pack ($7.99), Twilight Princess Pack ($7.99), Majora's Mask Pack ($7.99) and Boss Pack ($2.99), as well as the exclusive Dark Link Costume for Link.
OCARINA OF TIME, TWILIGHT PRINCESS, SKYWARD SWORD AND DEMON KING COSTUMES.
Each pack costs $0.99 + Tax and are not included in the Season pass. The Ocarina of Time, Twilight Princess and Skyward Sword packs consist of 2 costumes for Link and Zelda, where the Demon King pack has Ocarina of Time and Twilight Princess costumes for Ganondorf.
MASTER QUEST PACK
Epona as a weapon for Link, an original design for her Lv. 1, Twilight Epona for Lv. 2 and Ocarina of Time Epona for Lv. 3. 5 New story episodes explain Cia, Volga and Wizzro's side of the story. The Master Quest Adventure Mode Map, has a new set of rules added to the familiar adventure more rules. There are also 8-Bit Weapons for some of the playable characters and Lv. 2 and 3 weapons for Cia, Volga and Wizzro. There are also alternate costumes for all playable characters.

  • Purple Tunic/Four Swords Recolor for Link
  • Oracle Impa Recolor for Impa
  • Hilda Recolor for Zelda
  • Cia Recolor for Lana
  • Black Recolor for Sheik
  • Darmani Recolor for Darunia
  • Lulu Recolor for Ruto
  • Shadow Beast Recolor for Midna
  • Gothic Lolita Recolor for Agitha
  • Fairy Queen Recolor for Fi
  • Shadow Beast Recolor for Zant
  • Sword of Demise Recolor for Ghirahim
  • Wind Waker Recolor for Ganondorf
  • Link Recolor for Cia (Masked, Unmasked, Hatless)
  • Volvagia (Zelda II) Recolor for Volga
  • Blue Wizzrobe Recolor for Wizzro

The two costumes for this pack are Guardian of Time Costumes for Lana and Cia.


TWILIGHT PRINCESS PACK


Twili Midna added as a playable character, she fights with the Mirror of Twilight. Lv. 1 is the Mirror of Shadows, Lv. 2 is the Mirror of Silence and Lv. 3 is the Mirror of Twilight. The Dominion Rod is added as Weapon for Zelda. Lv. 1 is the Old Dominion Rod, Lv. 2 is the High Dominion Rod and Lv. 3 is the Royal Dominion Rod. The Twilight Princess Adventure Mode Map has a new set of rules added to the familiar adventure mode rules. There are also 8-Bit Weapons for some of the playable characters and more alternate costumes for all playable characters except Twili Midna.

  • Gold Tunic for Link
  • Skyward Sword Recolor for Impa
  • A Link to the Past Recolor for Zelda
  • Link Recolor for Lana
  • Impa Recolor for Sheik
  • Darbus Recolor for Darunia
  • Rutella Recolor for Ruto
  • Yellow Recolor for Midna
  • Sweet Lolita Recolor for Agitha
  • Light Spirit Recolor for Fi
  • Phantom Zant Recolor for Zant
  • Damaged Self Recolor for Ghirahim
  • Demise Recolor for Ganondorf
  • Lana Recolor for Cia (Masked, Unmasked, Hatless)
  • Lu Bu Recolor for Volga
  • Freezard Recolor for Wizzro
  • 8-Bit Hyrule Field: TP can now be used for BGM.

The two costumes for this pack are the Postman costume for Link, and Ilia costume for Zelda, both from the Twilight Princess game.

MAJORA'S MASK


Includes:
Young Link with the Fierce Deity's Mask. Lv. 2 and 3 weapons can be unlocked, being the Lv. 2 Furious Deity Mask and Lv. 3 Vengeful Deity Mask. Tingle with his Rosy Balloon. Lv. 2 and 3 weapons can be unlocked, being the Lv. 2 Love-Filled Balloon and Lv. 3 Mr. Fairy Balloon. These can be found also in the Termina Map. The Termina Map adds competition rules, and also has new costumes, being the wearable masks from Majora's Mask for every warrior (with the exception of Tingle, Young Link and Twili Midna). There are more 8-Bit Weapons to be unlocked as well in the Termina Map.

  • Fierce Deity Mask for Link
  • Mask of Truth for Impa
  • Bunny Hood for Zelda
  • Deku Mask for Lana
  • Kafei's Mask for Sheik
  • Goron Mask for Darunia
  • Zora Mask for Ruto
  • Ordon Shield for Midna
  • Don Gero's Mask for Agitha
  • Stone Mask for Fi
  • Circus Leader's Mask for Zant
  • Kamaro's Mask for Ghirahim
  • Odolwa's Remains for Ganondorf
  • Majora's Mask for Cia
  • Giant's Mask for Volga
  • Captain's Hat for Wizzro

Three new tracks will be available to use as BGM, being 8-Bit versions of the First, Second and Final Day songs heard in Clock Town.


BOSS PACK

Includes:
Boss Challenge - Challenge yourself in relentless battles against giant bosses to unlock new costumes for Link, Zelda, and Lana.
Ganon's Fury - Play as Ganon, rain chaos and destruction upon the battlefield, and unlock new costumes for Ganondorf and Cia.
